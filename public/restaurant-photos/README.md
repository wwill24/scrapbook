# Restaurant Photos

Add your restaurant photos to this directory with the following filenames:

- `french-laundry.jpg` - The French Laundry
- `blue-bottle.jpg` - Blue Bottle Coffee
- `sushi-nakazawa.jpg` - Sushi Nakazawa
- `farmhouse-kitchen.jpg` - Farmhouse Kitchen
- `tartine.jpg` - Tartine Bakery
- `state-bird.jpg` - State Bird Provisions

## Image requirements:
- Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- Size: Landscape orientation works best (400x300px or larger)
- Quality: High resolution for crisp display
- Content: Photos of the restaurant, food, or you both at the restaurant

The images will be displayed as 400x300px cards with hover effects and date badges.
