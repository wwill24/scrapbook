# Timeline Photos

Add your timeline milestone photos to this directory with the following filenames:

- `we-met.jpg` - Photo from when you first met
- `first-date.jpg` - Photo from your first date
- `first-kiss.jpg` - Photo from your first kiss moment
- `i-love-you.jpg` - Photo from when you first said "I love you"
- `first-trip.jpg` - Photo from your first trip together
- `one-year.jpg` - Photo from your one year anniversary

## Image requirements:
- Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- Size: Square images work best (300x300px or larger)
- Quality: High resolution for crisp display

The images will be displayed as 64x64px squares with rounded corners and shadow effects in the timeline.
